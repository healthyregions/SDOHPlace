**The SDOH & Place Symposium** is a meeting for SDOH researchers, practitioners, and advocates to share their work on measuring and mapping SDOH, linking public health with geography, GIScience, and social science. The 3rd Annual SDOH & Place Symposium will be held in downtown Chicago from September 23-September 25th, 2026. 

The Symposium is an intentionally smaller event, with sixty attendees maximum, to ensure meaningful exchange, robust discussions, and thoughtful networking. The latest cohort of [SDOH & Place Fellows](https://sdohplace.org/fellows) mapping dashboard prototypes to advance health equity. Representatives from academic, public, and private sectors share developments in their own research on measuring the social determinants of health at neighborhood-level, advances in linking design thinking with health equity research, and brainstorming what's needed for brighter futures.



## Submit an Abstract and Register for the Symposium!

Abstract submissions are now open! We're looking for presentations that engage with health equity in communities, measure or operationalize the social determinants of health & structural drivers of health at neighborhood scales, integrate design practices in novel ways in health equity research, or integrate public health with GIScience & geography in novel or unexpected ways.\
