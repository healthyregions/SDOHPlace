**Join us this September as we foster community, inspire meaningful dialogue, and explore the Social Determinants of Health & Place.**

* Wednesday, September 23rd - Friday, September 25th
* The Chicago Loop, Illinois 

The [SDOH & Place Project](https://sdohplace.org/project) works to build a community of practice around defining & using community-level social determinants of health (SDOH). We are accomplishing this through discussions on defining SDOH and using SDOH data, engaging new types of collaborations and applications with design justice, and breaking ground in developing new types of infrastructures & tools to support this work.

**The SDOH & Place Symposium** is a meeting for SDOH researchers, practitioners, and advocates to share their work on measuring and mapping SDOH, linking public health with geography, GIScience, and social science. 

At each symposium, [SDOH & Place Fellows](https://sdohplace.org/fellows) share their progress on developing data dashboards for health equity with human-centered design and spatial data. **Keynotes and speakers** from across the country push our thinking further to advance health equity initiatives and research, and together we break bread, celebrate achievements, and learn from each other.

At this year's **2026 SDOH & Place Symposium**, our growing community will come together to like never before.
