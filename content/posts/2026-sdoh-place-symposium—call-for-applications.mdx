**The SDOH & Place Symposium** is a meeting for SDOH researchers, practitioners, and advocates to share their work on measuring and mapping SDOH, linking public health with geography, GIScience, and social science. The 3rd Annual SDOH & Place Symposium will be held in downtown Chicago from September 23-September 25th, 2026. 



At each symposium, [SDOH & Place Fellows](https://sdohplace.org/fellows) share their progress on developing data dashboards for health equity with human-centered design and spatial data. **Keynotes and speakers** from across the country push our thinking further to advance health equity initiatives and research, and together we break bread, celebrate achievements, and learn from each other.

At this year's **2026 SDOH & Place Symposium**, our growing community will come together to like never before.
